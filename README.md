# Essity (essity)

Essity is a Swedish-headquartered global hygiene and health company operating across four business areas: Health & Medical, Personal Care, Consumer Tissue, and Professional Hygiene. The company is listed on Nasdaq Stockholm (ESSITY) and owns roughly 40 brands including TENA (incontinence care), Tork (professional hygiene), Libero (baby diapers), Libresse / Bodyform / Nana / Saba / Nuvenia / Libra (feminine care), Modibodi and Knix (period underwear), JOBST (compression hosiery), Leukoplast (adhesive bandages), Cutimed, Sorbact, Hydrofera and Actimove (wound care, sports medicine, orthopedic supports), Delta-Cast (casting), and consumer tissue brands Lotus, Zewa, Tempo, Cushelle, Plenty, Velvet, Regio, Sorbent and Purex. No public developer-facing APIs or developer portal have been identified for Essity; this repository tracks corporate, brand, sustainability, investor, and integration surfaces for ongoing API discovery. The company's Tork brand ships a connected washroom data platform — Tork Vision Cleaning — which is the closest thing to a public data surface in the portfolio.

## Business Areas

Essity reports across four business areas:

1. **Health & Medical** — TENA, JOBST, Leukoplast, Cutimed, Sorbact, Hydrofera, Actimove, Delta-Cast, Issviva
2. **Personal Care** — feminine care (Libresse, Bodyform, Nana, Saba, Nosotras, Nuvenia, Libra, TOM Organic, Modibodi, Knix), baby care (Libero, Lotus Baby, Familia, Pequeñín)
3. **Consumer Tissue** — Lotus, Zewa, Tempo, Cushelle, Plenty, Velvet, Regio, Sorbent, Purex, Handee, Colhogar, Okay, Orchid, Demakup, FEH, Viti
4. **Professional Hygiene** — Tork (including the Tork Vision Cleaning connected washroom platform)

## APIs

No public developer-facing APIs have been documented yet. Notable adjacent surfaces:

- **Tork Vision Cleaning** — IoT/data-driven cleaning platform for professional washrooms (sensors, dashboards). Not a public API today, but the most plausible future public data surface in the portfolio.
- **Knix** and **Modibodi** — direct-to-consumer e-commerce brands with Shopify-style storefronts; storefront APIs not publicly documented.

Contributions welcome — open an issue or PR if you find a public API surface for any Essity brand.

## Sustainability

Essity aligns its sustainability strategy to the **UN Sustainable Development Goals**, the **UN Global Compact**, and the **UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights**. Climate work is "guided by science-based targets" with five advancement areas: well-being, climate action, healthy ecosystems, empowered workforce, and responsible business.
